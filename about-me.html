About Me

My background is mostly in the arts: both fine arts and design. I studied painting, life drawing, photography, and graphic design in college before focusing on film production for a spell. I'm proficient with oil/acrylic paints, most DSLR cameras, the Adobe suite, Final Cut Pro, and any kind of drawing/sketching. One of my specialties is hand typography, which is a passion of mine. In any project I take on, my eye for design comes into play. I have always been able to use my creativity as well as my proclivity for technical problem solving to uniquely approach tasks.

I got started with computer programming as a teen, teaching myself how to code custom MySpace profiles for myself and friends. Years later I began to delve into it again, while employed as an intern at a small start-up tech company here in Portland. There I learned to work with HTML/Haml, CSS (particularly SASS), JavaScript, jQuery, Bootstrap, and Ruby on Rails. I learned extremely fast, and was soon hired on full-time as a front-end web developer.

Being able to excell at both technical and creative tasks gives me and my skills great marketability, something I am working on increasing every day.
